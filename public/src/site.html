About Tyler Alexandra

A boutique business with an international clientele, tyler alexandra is an intimately run, hands-on endeavor with access to the finest resources.

Tyle Alexandra was born out of designer TYLER ELLIS' DESIRE TO CREATE A TIMELESS HANDBAG - SOMETHING WOMEN COULD WEAR FOR YEARS BEFORE SUCCUMBING TO THEIR DAUGHTERS' PLEAS TO PASS IT DOWN. WITH THAT TREND-DEFYING GOAL IN MIND, TYLER ALEXANDRA LAUNCHED IN THE FALL OF 2010.

TYLER'S DESIGNS - MODERN INTERPRETATIONS OF CLASSIC SILHOUETTES - ARE BROUGHT TO LIFE IN ITALY BY THE WORLD'S TOP ARTISANS. MARRYING ULTRA LUXE MATERIALS - LIKE RICH LEATHER AND POLISHED CROCODILE - TO A SHARP AESTHETIC, EACH HANDBAG REPRESENTS AND ARDENT COMMITMENT TO COUTURE QUALITY CRAFTSMANSHIP.

About Tyler Ellis

INSPIRED BY HER LATE FATHER, PERRY ELLIS, AND HIS IMPACT ON AMERICAN FASHION, TYLER UNDERTOOK HER OWN DESIGN ENTERPRISE AFTER ATTENDING BOSTON UNIVERSITY. IN HONOR OF HER FATHER, THE DISTINCTIVE TYLER ALEXANDRA LOGO IS BASED ON A HANDWRITTEN ARTIFACT PENNED BY ELLIS HIMSELF BEFORE HE PASSED AWAY.

TYLER RESIDES BETWEEN NEW YORK AND LOS ANGELES WITH HER HUSBAND, BEN, AND THEIR TRIO OF PUPS.
